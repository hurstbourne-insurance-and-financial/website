Your Guide to Dental Insurance

Discover how dental insurance can help you maintain your smile and protect your health and finances.

What is preventive care?

Preventive care includes regular checkups, cleanings, and exams to maintain your dental health.

What is covered?

  • Preventive care like exams, cleanings, and X-rays.
  • Basic procedures like fillings and extractions.
  • Major services like crowns and root canals.

What is a "dental discount plan"?

  • Not actual insurance.
  • You pay the full cost of care.
  • Limited choice of dentists.
  • No out-of-network benefits.

Benefit of Preventive Care

Regular checkups are easier and less expensive than tooth restoration. Stop the problem before it begins.
