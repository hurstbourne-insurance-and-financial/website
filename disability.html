Common Misconceptions About Disability Insurance

"I'm young and healthy"
Disability isn't limited to age or poor health. Accidents can happen to anyone.
“Workers' compensation will cover me.”
Workers' comp applies only to work-related injuries, which represent a smaller percentage of disabilities.
“I have savings.”
Even substantial savings can deplete quickly during extended periods without income.
